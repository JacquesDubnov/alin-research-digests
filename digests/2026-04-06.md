### Impact of Fezolinetant on Work Productivity and Indirect Costs
- **Authors:** Morga et al.
- **Journal:** Menopause, 2026
- **PMID:** 41915440
- **Domain:** Query 3 — fezolinetant / non-hormonal / vasomotor
- **Relevance to ALIN:** Directly quantifies the workplace burden of vasomotor symptoms and economic benefit of fezolinetant, strengthening ALIN's ability to frame treatment as an investment in daily functioning.

### Hot Flashes Linked to Linguistic Markers of Cognitive Impairment
- **Authors:** Shaklai et al.
- **Journal:** Menopause, 2026
- **PMID:** 41401206
- **Domain:** Query 1 — HRT / menopause
- **Relevance to ALIN:** Connects vasomotor symptoms directly to cognitive complaints in a novel way (social media language analysis), supporting ALIN's holistic framing of hot flashes as more than a thermoregulatory issue.
